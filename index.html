Get to know me ...

I’m Carmen den Otter, a first-year student in Graphic and Digital Media (2024–2025) at the University College in Antwerp. As a half-Spanish, half-Dutch creative living in Belgium, I combine a caring nature with a strong sense of vision.

I am skilled in planning, organizing, and maintaining structure, and I work fluently with Figma and Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects). My main interests lie in branding and visual design.

Outside of my studies, I am fashion-conscious, follow social media trends, enjoy staying active, and am a true animal lover. In my free time, I enjoy spending quality moments with friends and family and find inspiration through travel and discovering new cultures.
